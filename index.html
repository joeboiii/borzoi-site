Borzois: The Silliest Creatures to Exist

Borzois are elegant yet utterly goofy. Here’s why they’re some of the silliest dogs around:

🐶 Their absurdly long snouts make them look permanently confused.
🐕 They have a tendency to stand in weird, dramatic poses for no reason.
🤣 Their zoomies are a chaotic mix of grace and complete nonsense.
👀 They stare deep into your soul with their beady eyes.
📏 They stretch to impossible lengths when lying down.
